Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

You'll need a valid driving license, ID proof (Aadhar/PAN), and address proof. For international customers, a valid passport and international driving permit are required.

What is the minimum rental period?

The minimum rental period is 1 hour. You can rent vehicles for hourly, daily, or weekly periods depending on your needs.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time without any cancellation charges.

Do you provide insurance coverage?

Yes, all our vehicles come with comprehensive insurance coverage. Additional insurance options are also available for extra protection.
